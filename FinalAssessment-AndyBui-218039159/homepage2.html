Wave

Composed by: Xuan Quynh


Fierce and gentle. Oh the wave passes, Standing before the waves, The waves must come from the wind.
Loud and silent, And the wave to come will be the same. I think of you and me And I wonder where the wind comes from,
The river doesn't understand itself. Hunger for love I think of the great sea. And I wonder
The wave doesn't find itself. Is strong in the heart. And I wonder where the waves come from. When will we love each other again?
When I go to the North, Out in the great sea Life is so long, How can I become
Or to the South, Thousands of waves are pushing, Years and months go by Like the hundreds of thousands of small waves
When I go anywhere, I think of you Which one never reaches the shore Like the sea, life is endless. In the great sea of love
My only direction. Even miles and miles from home? Clouds fly to the distant horizon. And lap forever against your shore?
