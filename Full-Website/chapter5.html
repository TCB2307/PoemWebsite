Chapter 5

When I go to the North,

Or to the South.

When I go anywhere, I think of you.

My only direction.

The world of Xuan Quynh and her lover is not just limited to North and South, there is always a permanent nostalgia of eternal love. Has love turned the world around? But there is only one way that she will never be forget, is her lover.


Love always goes along with anxiety and disturbances. Nevertheless, the poet still believes in her kindness and sincere love to overcome all as cloudy as those years.
